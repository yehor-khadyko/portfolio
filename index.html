Core Focus

What I Do

  • Systemic Interaction Designing engaging rules, progression curves, and balancing interlocking game loops where mechanics complement each other.
  • Game Feel & Impact Fine-tuning weight, velocity, timings, and responsive mechanical feedback to maximize player immersion and satisfaction.
  • Atmosphere & Level Design Structuring layouts to create specific combat and exploration scenarios, guiding player sightlines, and building intense environmental mood.

What I Don't Do

  • Coding I prefer visual scripting, logic assembly, and system architecture design over low-level codebase programming.
  • 3D & Asset Art I do not model or paint. I use functional assets as essential tools to test mechanics, scale, and layout pacing.
  • VFX & Final Animation I concentrate on gameplay pacing, constraints, and impact feel, leaving final visual polish to specialized artists.
